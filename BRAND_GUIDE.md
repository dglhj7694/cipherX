# Aroven Brand Guide

## Chosen Direction
- Final brand: `Aroven`
- Tone: premium, calm, intelligent
- Hidden idea: direction, angle, and disciplined judgment without saying "finance" out loud
- Logo cue: the `V` becomes the compass needle inside a restrained geometric mark
- Tagline: `Clarity before conviction`

## 12 Coined-Style Candidates
1. `Aroven` - sharp but calm; good balance of guidance and polish
2. `Velora` - smooth and premium; slightly softer and more lifestyle-like
3. `Orivan` - structured and confident; feels like a research tool brand
4. `Norive` - subtle directional feel with a lighter tone
5. `Lorven` - compact and elegant; a little more reserved
6. `Elvora` - polished and fluid; slightly more feminine in tone
7. `Veruno` - modern and clean; a touch more technical
8. `Avelin` - refined and approachable; less distinctive as a core mark
9. `Rovena` - memorable and soft; slightly more consumer-facing
10. `Noreva` - premium and airy; less anchored than the finalists
11. `Alvora` - stable and high-end; not as crisp in small wordmark use
12. `Orlune` - poetic and atmospheric; weaker product/tool fit

## Top 3 Finalists

### 1. Aroven
- Meaning feel: a quiet mix of arrow, orbit, and vantage without being literal
- Pronunciation: easy in Korean and English, with a strong center on the `v`
- Why it fits: sounds like a premium analysis product, not a coin or game
- Tagline: `Clarity before conviction`
- Logo link: use the `V` as the compass needle, with a circular orbit ring around it

### 2. Velora
- Meaning feel: velocity softened into elegance
- Pronunciation: smooth and easy, but more polished than analytical
- Why it fits: premium and memorable, though slightly softer than ideal for this product
- Tagline: `See the turn before the crowd`
- Logo link: slim north-star pin with a flowing orbital ring

### 3. Orivan
- Meaning feel: orient + advance, framed as a coined brand
- Pronunciation: strong and credible, with a more structured tone
- Why it fits: feels trustworthy and product-ready, though less playful than Aroven
- Tagline: `A steadier angle on the market`
- Logo link: offset axis lines with a centered directional pointer

## Selection Rationale
- `Aroven` won because it feels like software first, brand second, and explanation third.
- It stays premium without sounding cold.
- It gives us a natural logo move: turning the `V` into the directional needle.
- It works for scanner, chart analysis, and AI report surfaces without sounding too broad or too gimmicky.

## Visual Direction
- Primary colors: deep navy, soft teal, pale steel, warm gold accent
- Symbol style: abstract compass, not a literal candlestick or shield
- Wordmark style: tight spacing, clean geometry, highlighted `v` accent
- UI use: symbol can stand alone as tab icon, sidebar mark, or report badge

## Notes
- These are coined-style naming directions, not trademark-cleared legal approvals.
- Before public launch, run a proper domain and trademark screen for the final shortlist.
